How to Play
MMA Grid | Fill the 3×3 Grid
1
Each cell requires a fighter who satisfies both the row category and the column category.
2

3
Each fighter can only be used once across the entire grid.
4
After a correct answer, you'll see a rarity % showing how many players picked that fighter.
5
Weight class categories mean the fighter competed at that weight class, not just for their career, but for specific fights where noted.
